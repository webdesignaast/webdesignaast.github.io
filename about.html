Hunia Tarek Tomoum

Course instructor huniatomoum@gmail.com

 

Graduated from AAST in 2008, and obtained her masters degree in 2012 from the Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalonia, Barcelona, Spain.
Working as a part-time teaching assistant in AAST since 2008, and in AUC since 2013. Worked in Alchemy Design office as an Architect and in EHAF consulting engineers as a senior Architect leading the Architecture design team.

Such as; "People: Biosensors for the City", "Matter, Materiality and Material organization in Architecture" and "Robotic Sand forming", Egypt +20 design week , 1st international Egypt Design +Industry workshop , 5th national Architectural students competition Cairo university, and Evolo Skyscraper competition where her project has been published in "EVOLO SKYSCRAPERS" book.
